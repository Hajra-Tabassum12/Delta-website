Delta Medical Laboratories

Delta Medical Laboratories is one of the first private reference laboratories in Saudi Arabia. It was founded in 1997 by a group of prominent Saudi Consultants to provide medical laboratory services to all private and public health care sectors. We are very pleased to provide high-quality medical services to the whole community in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
We offer a broad range of routine and high complexity testing services used by the medical profession in the screening diagnosis, treatment follow-up, and treatment selection of several medical conditions. The company’s customers include government hospitals, government agencies, private hospitals, polyclinics independent physicians and physician groups, pharmaceutical companies, and other company firms.
